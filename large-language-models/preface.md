From the preface, we are going to learn all about LLMs, from the internal workings to practical applications. Learn about word representations and transformer architecture.
inner working - architecture, training methods, fine-tuning techniques.
We also examine various applications, such as text classification, clustering, topic modelling, chatbots, and search engines.

This book provides an introduction to the world of LLMs

representational vs generative LLMs
representational means to do a certain task like classification (that do not generate text)
genreratiom sla ones generate text like the GPT model

The book is divided into 3 parts - 

### 1. Understanding language models


We will see the inner workings of models both large and small.
Then we will go over the overview and common techniques, and then we will focus on 2 central components of these models: tokenization and embeddings.
We finish this part with an extended version of Jay's famous architecture of the illustrated transformer.
